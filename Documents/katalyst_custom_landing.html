The honest truth

Why most people feel stuck

Think about this for a second. You're not lazy. You're not broken. You've just been running on empty for so long that you've forgotten what fully alive feels like. For most people, it comes down to three quiet absences.

Lack of Energy

You wake up already tired. Coffee carries you to noon, willpower drags you to night, and the dreams you once had feel too heavy to lift. It was never a flaw in you — no one ever taught you how to generate energy on purpose.

Lack of Purpose

You've achieved things. On paper, it should be enough. So why does the morning feel grey? Without a reason that pulls you forward, even success starts to feel like standing still in expensive shoes.

Lack of Connection

You're surrounded by people and still feel unseen. The conversations stay shallow. What you really crave is connection that reaches the real you — and that kind of closeness was never meant to be found. It's built.

The 360° Method

The three pillars of 360° wellness

Real wellness isn't one thing fixed in isolation. It's your whole life finally moving in the same direction. Everything I teach stands on three pillars — and together, they hold up a life that feels like yours again.

Pillar 01

Congruence

Become aligned with the highest version of yourself. When what you think, what you say, and what you do finally point the same way, life stops being a fight against yourself — and starts becoming a force.

Pillar 02

Connection

Create deeper relationships. The quality of your life is the quality of your relationships. Learn to open, to truly listen, to be fully present — and watch every bond around you grow roots you can stand on.

Pillar 03

Contribution

Create meaning through service. The fastest road to a fulfilled life is making someone else's better. Purpose isn't something you stumble upon — it's something you give, through what you choose to contribute.

My Story

I had everything the world calls success. And I was empty.

For years, I chased the picture everyone told me to want. The career. The recognition. The applause. From the outside, I had arrived. Inside, I was running on empty.

Then came a season where everything I'd built seemed to crack at once. The success didn't save me. The noise didn't fill me. And one quiet morning I realised the man I'd become was not the man I wanted to be.

That breakdown became the beginning. I stopped looking for more out there and started doing the work in here — rebuilding my energy, my relationships, my reason for being alive. Piece by piece, I came home to myself.

What I found wasn't a secret. It was a system — the same three pillars I now teach. They gave me my life back, and they've since given thousands of people theirs.

So I'll make you the same promise I made myself that morning: never give up. Not on your energy. Not on the people you love. Not on yourself. I'm living proof of what becomes possible the moment you refuse to.

— Edoardo

Anyone who feels they're capable of more than they're currently living. Whether you're successful and quietly empty, or stuck and searching, the work meets you exactly where you are.
Do I need any experience?
None at all. The masterclass is built so a complete beginner can start today and go as deep as they choose. There's nothing to catch up on — you begin where you are.
How long do I have access?
Lifetime. Enrol once and return whenever you need it — the modules are yours to keep, revisit, and grow into for as long as you like.
How are the lessons delivered?
Through beautiful, on-demand video lessons you can watch on any device, at your own pace, from anywhere in the world. No live schedule to keep up with.
How much time does it take each week?
As little as 20–30 minutes a day. The rituals are designed to fit into a real, busy life — not to replace it.
What if I'm not disciplined enough to finish?
Motivation is one of the very things we rebuild together. The structure, the rhythm, and the community are designed to carry you on the days your willpower runs low.

No. This is a personal-development masterclass — a system for building energy, purpose and connection. It isn't a substitute for medical or psychological care, and if you're struggling with your mental health, please also reach out to a qualified professional.
Can I go through it with my partner?
Many people do. The relationship and connection pillars become even more powerful when you grow alongside someone you love.
What if it turns out not to be for me?
If you go through the first modules, genuinely do the work, and don't feel the shift, reach out to our team and we'll make it right. Your transformation matters more to us than any transaction.
